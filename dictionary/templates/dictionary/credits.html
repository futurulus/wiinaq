Acknowledgments from the Alutiiq/Sugpiaq Dictionary

These draft acknowledgments, by Joe Kwaraceius, are part of the 2018 preprint of Kwaraceius et al. (2018). He has requested that they be included in publicly available electronic files.

This dictionary would have never gone anywhere without the the help of many other people. I haven’t had a steady job for over 6 years now, but odd jobs have helped me to continue anyway. The food and meals really, really counted when I had to stretch my own rations. Goes to show you all, that it takes a community to get things done, especially when circumstances get difficult. The friendship, psychological support and kindnesses of all below are heartily appreciated.
